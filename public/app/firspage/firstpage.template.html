Interface description:

Scope - group of articles

- options where you can : Log In / Sign Up , Create post , go to Home page.

Sort by - enable filters (applied only on your current scope of articles).

- button to load new scope of articles (disappeared when you are sorting).

X - button to disable filters (appeared when you are sorting).

- rules and information about website.

Edit - appear under only your articles after you log in.

Click on article to open content.

Click on chat in right bottom angle to open chat window.
