  • Follow this rules

Interface description:

Scope - group of articles

- options where you can : Log In / Sing Up , Create post , go to Home page.

- button for load new scope of articles (disappeared when you are sorting your scope of articles).

X - button for disable filters (appeared when you are sorting your scope of articles).

- rules and information about website (GitHub ,Tools used).

Sort by - enable filters (applied only on your current scope of articles).

Edit - appear under your article after log in

Click on article to open content.

Click on chat in right bottom angle to open chat window.

Writing

Writing articles do not take a lot of time , just log in and press in right top menu button 'Create Post'

Reading

Absolute open content every one can read article other people with out any restrictions.

Chatting

Chat is place where you can communicate with other people in real time . Chat available in right bottom of screen.
