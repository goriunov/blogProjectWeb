What is ScBlog ?

ScBlog is a blog where you can broadcast anything. You can read and write articles,chat,create your own profile while you follow the simple rules mentioned below. All content provided on this blog is for informational purpose only. As well this project is open source therefore anyone can look, copy code.

Our blog Rules:

  • Do not publish articles which contain inappropriate content.

  • Be nice to other people in chat.

  • Please follow these rules.

Interface description:

Scope - group of articles

- options where you can : Log In / Sign Up , Create post , go to Home page.

Sort by - enable filters (applied only on your current scope of articles).

- button to load new scope of articles (disappeares when you are sorting).

X - button to disable filters (appeares when you are sorting).

- rules and information of/about this website.

Edit - appear under only your articles after you log in.

Click on article to open content.

Click on chat in right bottom corner to open chat window.

Writing

Does not take long time to make a post.

1)Log in.

2)Press in right top menu button 'Create Post'.

3)Write your article.

Reading

Read what others post in different categories (Animation, Comics, Films, Games, Programming, Serial, Technology, or Other) all this categories are available in Sort by menu.

Chatting

Chat with other people in real time , chat is available in right bottom corner. Press on chat window to open it (You must be sign in to use chat).
